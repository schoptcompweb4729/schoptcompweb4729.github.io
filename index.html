About

Plantable is a a home decorations/home furniture company making plant stands. We started our company on November, 2022. Plantable is a Junior Achivement (JA) company, which is a company made by kids as an option in school. Plantable is located in Canada, Alberta. Plantable's plant stands come are fit to any size plant: small, medium, or large, they're all the same price.
